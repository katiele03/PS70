Course Introduction

This page serves as an introduction to PS70: Intro to Digital Fabrication. Throughout the semester, I will document my weekly assignments, design process, and final project development. The goal of this course is to explore hands-on fabrication techniques while building functional electromechanical systems.

I am particularly interested in combining mechanical design, electronics, and human-centered engineering. My final project will focus on building a dynamic grip strengthener that adapts resistance based on user input, with applications in rehabilitation and physical therapy.

Katie Le | Harvard University
