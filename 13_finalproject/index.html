# Final Project Idea: Booming Battle Board 🚢💥 --- ## Traditional Battleship - Each player has two grids in front of them: - **Own board** → ships are placed and marked when hit - **Tracking board** → tracks opponent’s coordinates attacked - Players take turns calling out coordinates to attack - A ship is **sunk** when all its cells are hit - Goal: sink all opponent’s ships ![Battle Example](../battle.jpg) --- ## My Twist on Battleship A two-player Battleship board with **LED feedback and sensors**. Instead of calling out coordinates, players tap directly on their grid. - Lights up **red** for “hit” - Lights up **white** for “miss” - Plays sound effects ![Ship Example](../ship.jpg) --- ## 3D Design and Fabrication - **Laser cutting** → engraved coordinate plates + frosted LED tiles - **3D printing** → ships + board casing --- ## Electronics (Input / Output) **Inputs** - Ship detection - Magnets + Hall sensors - Conductive foil + capacitive pads - Capacitive touch grid → detects tapped cells **Outputs** - RGB LEDs under each tile - 🔴 Red = hit opponent’s ship - ⚪ White = miss - 🟢 Green = ship on own board - Sounds - 💥 Explosion = hit - 💦 Splash = miss --- ## Microcontroller Programming - Detects when a player taps a tile - Detects when a ship is present - Turns LEDs on/off - Plays sounds ---
