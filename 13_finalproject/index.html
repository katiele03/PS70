<div class="textcontainer"> <p class="margin"></p> <h3>Final Project Idea 1: Booming Battle Board 🚢💥 </h3> </div> ### Traditional Battleship - A two player game where each player has two grids in front of them: - their own board, where their ships varying in sizes on the grid horizontally or vertically are placed and where they mark when they’ve been hit - their tracking board, where they mark the coordinates they’ve attacked their opponent - The two players take turn “attacking” each other’s ships by picking coordinates where they think the other player has placed their ships - The objective of the game is to sink your opponent’s ships - If the ship lies in the cell/coordinate, it is a hit - If the ship does not lie in the cell/coordinate, it is a miss - A ship is sunk when all cells the ship lies in has been hit - Players keep track of the cells that are called on the grid in front of them <div style="text-align:center; margin-top:40px;"> <img src="./battle.jpg" style="max-width:600px; width:100%; height:auto;"> <p class="caption" style="color:black;">Traditional Battleship</p> </div> ### My Twist on Battleship
